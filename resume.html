Procter & Gamble - Maintenance Technician

Sept 2023 - Present

Mehoopany, PA

Procter & Gamble's plant in Mehoopany PA is where I currently work, as a Technician responsible for working with my team to keep the line running at optimal condition to produce diapers. If you've had reason to look at a box of Pampers recently, there's a strong chance I had a hand in making it.

This job has demanded a lot of my technical skills, and has drastically improved my mechanical functions. In addition to being responsible for the loading of material to keep the line operational, all technicians on the team are responsible for troubleshooting the equipment should the line fail.

There are many complicated and line-specific pieces of hardware inside the line, but we work primarily around optic sensors, motors, pnuematics, FIFEs, probes, HMIs, glue applicators, and of course all the bolts and adhesives that hold it all together. Not only working around such, but understanding their use at a foundational level, to the point where if there is an issue on the line, we're able to quickly troubleshoot the source of the problem and see that it gets resolved.

In my position I am responsible for overseeing all of the glues that go into forming the diaper, as well as the areas of machinery that produce what we call the "patch" and "core" of the diaper. Quality Control is a heavy focus at P&G, and that falls on my shoulders as well regarding these adhesives. Since my time there, I've also received an accolade for "Best Equipment Owner" for the above mentioned area not just on my line, but across 48 other possible recipients.

C3i Solutions - Hardware / Logistics Technician

Feb 2020 - Sept 2023

Pittston, PA

C3i Solutions is a company that provides different medical devices to their clients. While working for them, I held two different positions in both Hardware and Logistics.

As a Hardware Technician, my job was to set up the medical devices purchased according to client specification. For different medical studies, there were different ways in which every device needed to operate under different conditions. I worked with different specific technologies such as oximeters and scales, as well as more common devices like iPads and iPhones.

Attention to detail was the most important required skill here. An incredibly large order could be turned worthless should a device not be set to exact specifications. Understanding how to read the work orders provided and become more intricately familiar with the devices in question was tantamount, and I received quite a lot of experience in doing so.

As a Logistics Technician, I filled a staffing need by working in the warehouse instead. Here our duties instead revolved around receiving the devices into the building. However the amount of care required was absolutely the same. I was responsible for logging all new equipment into our database, transferring equipment, staging it for use by the hardware division, the physical storage of such equipment. More than any other skill, my use of Excel had gotten extremely polished at this point, along with the distribution of responsibility in a team setting.

Sportech Industries - Tote Technician

Aug 2018 - Jan 2021

Mohegan Sun - Wilkes Barre, PA

As part of the Mohegan Sun Casino, I worked for Sportech, the third-party used to run the technical aspects of the racetrack. I was responsible for the maintenance of and the resolution of all issues regarding the sports betting machines used to place wagers on horse races, as well as troubleshoot and fix issues with equipment such as televisions, and different specific hardware involved in the large billboard display on the racetrack.

The most prominent skill in this position revolved around logical problem solving. Most of the issues we faced came from customer reports on a piece of hardware not responding correctly, or a different issue regarding their wager, such as losing their ticket. A very important part of our responsibilities was to parse these customer statements, acknowledge and understand the situation, and use the information at hand to come to an apt solution.

Specific work done in this position involved the repair and replacement of esports betting hardware, along with heavy usage of our database and SQL functions.
