About Me

Hello! More than anything, I need to profess my enormous love of technology.

I'm enamored by technology, and grew up alongside it. No matter what stage of growth I was in, my interests and passions all seemed to revolve around the electronic. Different games and the pieces of hardware they ran on fascinated me, and it didn't take long for me to realize just how much I wanted to know how they worked.

That spurred my interest in furthering my education, and to this day I've been bolstering my skills in related fields, gaining more mechanical know-how along the way. Now I'm looking to put every effort into the world of software, where the skills that I've been polishing can really shine.
